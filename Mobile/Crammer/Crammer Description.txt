Crammer is a clever memorizing tool helping you quickly move information from short-term to long-term memory.  
Build up dictionaries of data you would like to memorize, and let the tool help you make the information stick. 
All you have to do is answer truthfully whether you remember an entry or not.  
Perfect for e.g. students in need of memorizing information, people learning new languages etc.
A sample dictionary with the countries of the world and their capitals will get you started.  
All entries can be sorted in various ways, shuffled etc. to challenge and improve memory retention.
